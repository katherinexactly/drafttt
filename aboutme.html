ABOUT ME

Learn more abour your master

Hi! I am Alisa

As a professional makeup, I'm skilled in applying cosmetics to enhance a person's appearance, often working in the fashion, entertainment, or beauty industry. I use makeup to create various looks, from natural to dramatic, and may specialize in areas like theatrical, special effects and bridal makeup.

As a professional hairstylist, I specialize in cutting, coloring, and styling hair. I work as a freelance artist and provide a range of services, including haircuts, blowouts, hair coloring, extensions, and various hairstyling techniques. My skills are essential in helping clients achieve the hairstyle they desire.

I would like to share a little about myself and my journey in the beauty industry

  • Start

    It all began when I was just 12 years old and started experimenting with makeup on myself and my friends.

    I have been in this field for almost 23 years now, which may sound scary to say, but it's true!

  • Education

    In the 10th grade, my mother sent me to a college of hairstyling in Kyiv to help me decide whether I wanted to pursue a career in this field. It was then that I became convinced that this was exactly what I wanted to do!

  • Post-Secondary Education

    After that, I enrolled in the All-Ukrainian School of Style and Makeup. In 2003, learned two diplomas, which confirmed my professional qualifications in field of hairstyling, makeup and designer.

  • Moving to Canada

    Become to Canada I still went to college to upgrade my skills.

    I completed 6 month courses at CMU college for Fashion Makeup/Hairstyle/SPFX.
